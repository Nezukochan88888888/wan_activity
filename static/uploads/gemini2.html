Purpose & Future

Our Mission

"DICT of the people and for the people." We commit to provide every Filipino access to vital ICT infostructure and services, ensure sustainable growth of ICT-enabled industries, and establish a One Digitized Government, One Nation.

Our Vision

"An innovative, safe and happy nation that thrives through and is enabled by Information and Communications Technology." We aspire for a progressive, safe, secured, contented, and happy Filipino nation.
